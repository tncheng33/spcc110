Leadership Forum — Sports

As part of the College’s 110th and 111th Anniversary celebrations across the 2025-2027 academic years, SPCC is launching a series of Leadership Forum events. This Sports session spotlights leadership in and through sport: building team culture, strengthening resilience under pressure, and turning discipline into service and impact. By hearing from athletes and sports leaders across generations, students and alumni gain practical insights into performance, character, and purpose-driven leadership on and off the field.
