Celebrating Legacy, Inspiring the Future

Embrace Love:
A Quest for Compassion

Join us as we celebrate 110 years of learning, service, and community spirit at St. Paul’s Co-educational College.

Welcome

In the spirit of Faith, Hope, and Love, we gather to celebrate our journey—110 years of excellence, resilience, and a steadfast commitment to the values that define us. These virtues have not only guided our path but have inspired our community to thrive and achieve extraordinary new milestones.

Our anniversary theme, “Embrace Love: A Quest for Compassion 愛己、愛人、愛世界”, encapsulates our collective mission. It calls upon us to recognize the profound power of love—not as an abstract ideal but as a transformative force. Love compels us to lead with empathy, to serve with humility, and to forge a community rooted in kindness and inclusivity.

As we reflect on this remarkable 110-year journey, we honor the past with gratitude, celebrate the present with purpose, and envision a future shaped by hope and compassion. This is a time to reaffirm our commitment to the values that unite us, to seek greater meaning in our shared endeavors, and to extend our impact beyond the SPCC family, contributing to a better and more harmonious world.

With Faith, Hope, and Love as our foundation and compassion as our guide, we stand poised to shape a legacy that is enduring, inclusive, and radiant with promise. Together, let us write the next chapter of our story, guided by the shared belief in the boundless potential of our community.
