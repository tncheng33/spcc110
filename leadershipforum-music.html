Leadership Forum — Music

St. Paul’s Co-educational College has upheld the Christian ethos of “Faith, Hope, and Love” for over a century. As part of the celebrations marking the College’s 110th and 111th Anniversaries during the academic years 2025 to 2027, SPCC is launching a series of Leadership Forum events to foster thoughtful dialogue and the exchange of ideas. Following the successful inaugural Sports session, this second session focuses on Music.

Moderator

Mr Patrick Yeung 楊偉添

Class of 1975

A seasoned executive with solid commercial and operational experience across Asia, and Professor of Practice (Tourism Marketing) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. With a longtime passion for classical music philanthropy, he has performed as a soloist in numerous charitable concerts across Asia and the US.

Panelists

Prof Sharon Choa 蔡敏德

Class of 1978

An accomplished conductor, musicologist and educator, and former Dean of the School of Music at the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts.

Ms Joyce Cheung 張貝芝

Class of 2010

An accomplished jazz pianist, performer, arranger and composer, and recipient of the Best Instrumental Album at the Golden Melody Awards.

Mr Elliot Leung 梁皓一

Class of 2013

A composer and conductor working in both Hollywood and China, signed to Sony Classical. His works are performed regularly by the New York Philharmonic and the Shanghai Symphony.
